Bildup AI Customer Privacy Policy

About Bildup

Bildup AI Services Limited (RC: 6949775) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our services.

We are dedicated to ensuring that your personal information is handled responsibly and in accordance with applicable data protection laws and regulations.

How Does This Policy Apply?

This Privacy Policy applies to all users of our services, including:

  • Website visitors and users
  • Mobile app users
  • Service subscribers
  • Anyone who interacts with our platform

By using our services, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

What Data Do We Collect?

We collect various types of information to provide and improve our services:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, billing address
  • Account Information: Username, password, profile details
  • Usage Data: How you interact with our services, features used
  • Communication Records: Messages, feedback, support interactions
  • Technical Data: Device information, IP address, browser type

How Do We Use This Information?

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • Providing and maintaining our services
  • Processing payments and managing accounts
  • Improving user experience and service quality
  • Sending important updates and notifications
  • Analyzing usage patterns and trends
  • Preventing fraud and ensuring security
  • Complying with legal obligations
  • Marketing and promotional activities (with consent)

Disclosing Your Personal Information

We may share your information in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: Third-party vendors who help us deliver services
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or government authorities
  • Terms Enforcement: To enforce our terms of service and policies
  • Fraud Prevention: To detect and prevent fraudulent activities
  • Asset Protection: In connection with business transfers or mergers
  • With Consent: When you explicitly authorize disclosure

Transfer of Personal Information to Foreign Country

We may transfer your personal information to other countries in accordance with the Nigerian Data Protection Regulation (NDPR):

  • Transfers to countries with adequate data protection laws
  • Transfers with appropriate safeguards and security measures
  • Transfers for specific purposes with your consent
  • Transfers necessary for contract performance
  • Transfers for legitimate business interests

We ensure that all international transfers comply with applicable data protection laws.

Choices and Rights of The Data Subject

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Rectification: Correct inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Erasure: Request deletion of your personal data
  • Data Portability: Receive your data in a structured format
  • Objection: Object to processing of your personal data
  • Restriction: Limit how we process your information
  • Withdrawal: Withdraw consent at any time

To exercise these rights, contact us at: support@bildupafrica.com

What Data Do We Retain?

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to:

  • Provide our services and maintain your account
  • Comply with legal and regulatory requirements
  • Resolve disputes and enforce agreements
  • Protect against fraud and security threats
  • Improve our services and user experience

Retention periods vary based on the type of data and its purpose. We regularly review and delete data that is no longer needed.

Security of Data

We implement comprehensive security measures to protect your personal information:

  • Physical Security: Secure data centers and facilities
  • Technical Security: Encryption, firewalls, and access controls
  • Organizational Security: Employee training and security policies
  • Regular Audits: Security assessments and vulnerability testing
  • Incident Response: Procedures for handling security breaches
  • Data Backup: Secure backup and recovery systems

Despite our efforts, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

Updates to Our Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or applicable laws. We will notify you of any material changes by:

  • Posting the updated policy on our website
  • Sending email notifications to registered users
  • Displaying prominent notices on our platform

We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect your information.

Age Restriction

Our services are not intended for persons under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

Consent

By using our services, you consent to the collection, use, and disclosure of your personal information as described in this Privacy Policy. You may withdraw your consent at any time by contacting us or adjusting your account settings.
